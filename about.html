ABOUT US

Welcome to Hyderabad Trekking Company (HTC)! We are passionate adventurers dedicated to bringing you the best trekking and outdoor experiences in and around Hyderabad. Our mission is to connect people with nature, promote eco-friendly travel, and create unforgettable memories.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of nature enthusiasts, HTC has grown into a vibrant community of trekkers and explorers. We organize regular treks, camping trips, and adventure activities for all age groups and skill levels.
